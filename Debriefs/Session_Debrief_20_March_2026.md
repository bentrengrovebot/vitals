## Days 1–30 Protocol

### Diet
- Eliminate refined carbohydrates, added sugar, and alcohol — primary triglyceride intervention
- Protein 160–180g per day, minimum 40g per meal, whole food sources only
- Carbohydrates from vegetables and fruit only in this phase — no bread, pasta, rice, grains
- Fat from whole food sources acceptable (eggs, meat, olive oil, avocado, nuts)
- No ultra-processed food enters the house — purchasing rule, not consumption rule
- No liquid calories beyond black coffee, tea, water
- Lunch pre-decided and prepared — remove the in-moment decision entirely
- Emotional eating buffer: 500ml water + 20-minute wait before any craving-driven food decision
- Pre-positioned high-protein snacks at home and work (hard boiled eggs, Greek yoghurt, clean beef jerky)

### Supplements — Week 1–2
- Vitamin B6 as P-5-P: 50mg daily (confirmed functional deficiency via organic acids)
- Magnesium glycinate: 400mg at night (sleep + kynurenate metabolism support)
- Quality B-complex: daily with food

### Supplements — Week 3–4 additions
- Omega-3 fish oil: 3–4g EPA/DHA combined daily (therapeutic dose for triglyceride reduction)
- Calcium-D-glucarate: 1000mg daily (elevated glucaric acid + elevated beta-glucuronidase)

### Training
- 3 days per week, full body, 45 minutes maximum
- Knee-safe movement selection:
  - Lower body push: Leg press (feet high on platform), Romanian deadlift, hip thrust — NO barbell squat, lunges, or high knee-flexion movements
  - Lower body pull: Romanian deadlift
  - Upper body push: Dumbbell or machine press
  - Upper body pull: Cable/machine row, lat pulldown
  - Core: Dead bug, pallof press — NO sit-ups or heavy spinal flexion
- Intent-based training (Pakulski) — feel the muscle, don't just move the weight
- Missed session rule: do not double up, return to schedule

### Sleep
- Single change only: fixed wake time 7 days per week
- Outdoor light within 30 minutes of waking (minimum 5 minutes)
